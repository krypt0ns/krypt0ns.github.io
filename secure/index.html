Account Protection Guide

Account Aging Process

We highly suggest aging the account on your IP for a recommended 7 days before playing a cup. You also want to avoid placing super high in cups especially if it's your first few. (qual is fine just not something crazy like top 10)

How to Prevent Pullbacks by 95%

Follow these critical steps:

  • Monitor email for recovery request notifications and cancel them immediately
  • Check support page for open tickets and close them
  • Change all account/email information (name, address)
  • Remove any payment options
  • Unlink external accounts
  • Change username if possible
  • Create and link new external accounts (Xbox, PSN, Nintendo)

Important Notice

Taking immediate action on these security measures is crucial for maintaining account stability. The first few days are especially critical for preventing recovery attempts.
